Introduction

SD2Alloy presents a novel automatic composition technique for sequence diagrams with the help of Alloy. The process of the composition works as follows: two given sequence diagrams are transformed in two Alloy models, which create logical constraints that uniquely characterise each model by restricting the metamodels and then combined according to a compositional glue which specifies how the models should be glued together to produce the intended composition
